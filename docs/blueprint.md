# **App Name**: Luz do Céu Kids

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Bundles: Display a comprehensive catalog of Christian coloring book packages (Basic, Premium) with detailed descriptions, included bonuses, and clear pricing.
- Secure Checkout Process: Facilitate a secure and streamlined payment flow supporting various methods (e.g., credit cards, PIX), ensuring customer data protection.
- Instant Digital Product Delivery: Upon successful purchase, provide immediate and convenient access to download all purchased coloring books and bonus digital content.
- Customer Account Portal: Allow registered users to log in and access all their purchased digital products and materials at any time, supporting the 'Acesso Vitalício' promise.
- AI Creative Prompt Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests biblical story or Christian value-based prompts to inspire new coloring page ideas for children and parents.
- FAQ and Testimonials Section: Present a well-organized section for frequently asked questions and display authentic customer testimonials to build trust and inform users.
- Direct WhatsApp Support Integration: Provide a readily accessible button or link to initiate direct customer support via WhatsApp for quick assistance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A serene and welcoming medium blue (#4F96CC). This hue, reminiscent of the sky and calm waters, evokes 'Luz do Céu' (Light of Heaven) while maintaining a gentle, inviting tone suitable for children and families. It offers good contrast on a light background.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#EEF3F6). This nearly white background provides a clean, open canvas that is visually soft and enhances readability, maintaining consistency with the primary hue for a harmonious palette.
- Accent color: A fresh and lively aqua-green (#42E5B9). Chosen to be analogous to the primary blue, this brighter, more saturated color creates energetic highlights for call-to-action buttons, key information, and interactive elements, adding a playful touch.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif). Its elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel is ideal for conveying the 'stories bíblicas' and 'valores cristãos' in a sophisticated yet approachable manner.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). A modern, neutral, and highly readable sans-serif font ensures clarity and legibility for all descriptions, benefits, and testimonials, complementing the headline font without distraction.
- Use custom, friendly, and simple illustrations that are playful and easily recognizable, echoing the themes of 'Luz do Céu Kids' and Christian values. Integrate existing emoji references like stars, hearts, and houses where appropriate for a consistent visual language.
- Implement a clean, spacious, and responsive layout, emphasizing ease of navigation and a positive user experience on both desktop and mobile devices. Organize content in clearly defined sections for product offers, benefits, testimonials, and FAQs.
- Incorporate subtle and delightful micro-animations for interactive elements, hover states, and transitions between sections. These should enhance user engagement without causing distraction, reflecting the app's 'lúdica e educativa' (playful and educational) nature.
